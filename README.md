## ⚙️ How to use the software

### Adding your project
Open the AudioSFX application. Drag your video file into the main window. The software reads your video and prepares the timeline.

### Selecting sound types
Choose the style of sound you need from the list. The AI analyzes the movement in your video. It suggests matching sounds for items like footsteps, impacts, or wind.

### Generating sounds
Click the Generate button. A progress bar shows you the remaining time. The AI creates audio tracks that fit your video length and style.

### Exporting your final video
Once you review the sounds, click the Export button. The software uses professional tools to combine your original video with the new sound layer. Your final product saves as a high-quality video file ready for sharing.

## 💡 Tips for better results

*   **Use clear footage**: The software works best when the action in the video is clear. High-definition source files produce better results.
*   **Check audio levels**: You can adjust the volume of the generated sounds in the sidebar. Balance the background audio with your new sound effects for a natural feel.
*   **Group your tracks**: Use the tag system to label your sounds. This helps you organize large projects with many layers.
